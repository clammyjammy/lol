how to make BOWDOIN's artichoke parmesan DIP!!!

ingredients

  • 14 oz. can artichoke hearts, drained
  • 1 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup low-fat mayonnaise
  • 1 clove (or more) garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp. lemon zest
  • dash cayenne pepper

steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Combine first six ingredients in a food processor, and process until smooth.
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste. Spread mixture evenly in a small oven-proof au gratin dish.
  4. Sprinkle with 1 Tbsp. grated parmesan cheese. Bake 15 minutes until top is golden, and serve warm. (Makes 1.5 cups)
